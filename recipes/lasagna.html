Resep Asparagus Lasagna

This asparagus lasagna recipe is a delicious and healthy twist on the classic dish, featuring fresh asparagus as the main ingredient. you have to try this recipe! before you make it, make sure you have all the ingredients ready. so let's get started!

Bahan-bahan:

  • 250g lasagna sheets
  • 1 cup asparagus, chopped
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instruksi:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan and sauté the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped asparagus and cook for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble the lasagna by layering the lasagna sheets, asparagus mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
